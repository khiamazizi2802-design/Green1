Green Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Green ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Green Nightlife mobile application. This Privacy Policy details our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our services under European GDPR and German DSGVO regulations.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our premium eco-luxury ride-sharing and VIP nightlife management systems, we collect the following data points:

  • Personal Identity Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture (stored securely in Firebase Auth and Firestore).
  • Real-time Telemetry & GPS Location: Precise location services are used to match passengers with nearest professional electric vehicle pilots and calculate accurate navigation routes.
  • Partner & Manager KYC documentation: Commercial registrations, driver permits, and identification proof (stored via encrypted Firestore buckets).
  • Financial Data (Stripe Connect): We integrate directly with Stripe Connect. Banking IBAN, tax IDs, and payouts are processed securely through Stripe's certified gateway. We do not store raw card numbers.

2. How We Use the Data

We process collected data to maintain service operations, including:

  • Routing and dispatching electric fleet shuttles.
  • Processing split-bill calculations inside friendship circles.
  • Enabling managers to configure VIP ticket options and menu items.
  • Enforcing legal compliance audits (GwG & DSGVO compliance rules).

3. Data Protection (GDPR / DSGVO)

We implement a strict DSGVO Anonymizer Shield. PII (Personally Identifiable Information) such as exact names, guest locations, and vehicle IDs are masked and tokenized within the administration command center to prevent unauthorized telemetry viewing. You retain the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time.

4. Contact Us

For questions or support regarding data protection, please contact the Green Operations team at support@green-2efb0.web.app.
